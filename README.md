## Salutations 👋

I am known as JgbSpark7, though some simply call me Ren. I make no claim to being remarkable; I am merely another traveler adrift in this strange procession of days, finding solace in the fact that none of us walk it alone. A trivial detail, if one insists on knowing: my fascination with computers began not from ambition or necessity, but from irritation. My Nintendo Switch struggled along at 30 FPS and suffered from abysmal ping, and in seeking to understand why, I wandered into the world of technology and never quite found my way back out.
